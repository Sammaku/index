Maajis Cookie Recipie

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter softened
  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 2 tsp hot water
  • 1 tsp vanilla essence
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 3 cups all purpose flour
  • 2 cups chololate chips

Instructions

  1. Cream together the butter and the sugar
  2. Beat in the eggs one at the time
  3. Add the vanilla essence
  4. Dissolve baking soda in to the hot water, add to the batter with salt
  5. Add the flour and the chocolte chips
  6. Pre-heat the oven at 180 degree celcius
  7. Bake in the pre-heated oven until edges are nicely brown around 13 minuts
